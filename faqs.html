  • What is gothere?

    gothere is a mobile (andriod and IOS) and web application that lets you find events and places especially those close to you. You are also able to get tickets for evets at your convenience.

  • How to book tickeks

    Events can be found by scrolling through the feed section of the app or using the search functions. To book a ticket to an event of your choice, simply click on the view more button of the event and purchase the ticket of your choice.

  • Where to find your tickets

    Once payment has been confirmed by are paymemt partner, a digital ticket would be generated with a QR code and downloaded to your device in a PDF format. you can choose to print this ticket
